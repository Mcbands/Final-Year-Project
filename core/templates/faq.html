Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Keres Coaching Institute?

Keres Coaching Institute is an online institute that aims to provide quality business education through online courses and coaching.

2. What courses does Keres Coaching Institute offer?

Keres Coaching Institute offers a range of business and IT courses such as marketing and advertising, financial management, human resources management, sales and negotiation skills, project management, entrepreneurship, and innovation, international business and trade, business communication and presentation skills, supply chain management, Web development, Computer Programming, Computer Networking, and more.

3. Are the courses self-paced or facilitated?

Keres Coaching Institute offers a mix of self-paced and facilitated courses. Students can choose the pace of their learning and still have access to instructors in facilitated courses.

4. Will I have access to instructors and other students during the course?

Yes, students have access to their instructors and a community of fellow students through the online learning platform.

5. How much do the courses cost?

The cost of the courses varies depending on the course and its duration. Keres Coaching Institute offers competitive prices for their courses.

6. How do I enroll in a course?

Enrolling in a course is an easy process. All you need to do is select the course you would like to take, register by creating an account, complete the payment process, and start learning.

8. What type of certificate will I receive after completing a course?

Upon completing a course from Keres Coaching Institute, students receive a certificate of completion that they can use to showcase their newly acquired skills or as proof of their professional development under TEVETA.
